Copyright & Usage
The Photographer retains full copyright to all images. Upon final payment, the Client receives a personal-use license to print and share the delivered images. Commercial use, resale, or altering of the images requires the Photographer's written consent. (See the accompanying Print & Copyright Release.)
6
Portfolio & Marketing Use
The Client [grants / does not grant] the Photographer permission to use images from the session for portfolio, website, and marketing purposes. (See the accompanying Model Release.)
7
Limitation of Liability
In the unlikely event of equipment failure, lost files, or other circumstances beyond the Photographer's control, the Photographer's liability is limited to a refund of amounts paid. Neither party is liable for failure to perform due to circumstances beyond their reasonable control.
8
Image Delivery & Archiving
Edited images are delivered via [online gallery] within [#] weeks of the session. The Photographer keeps files for [#] months after delivery as a courtesy; the Client is responsible for downloading and backing up their images.
